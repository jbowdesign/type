History of the font

Designed by Eduardo Tunni and Mohamed Gaber. This font is a modern multi-script sans-serif display font. It is inspired by fluid and soft shapes of hand-painted street shop signage. The common usage is for website headlines, UI, social media branding, Arabic language content and, signage and advertising.

Supported Languages include:English. Arabic, Farsi and Urdu within the Arabic script. Other latin-based languages.
